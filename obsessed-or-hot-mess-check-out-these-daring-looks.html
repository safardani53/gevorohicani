INSECT EMBELLISHMENT

Illusion netting is so called because it gives off the illusion of bare skin. In this case, the detailing on Charlize Theron’s dress gives the illusion there are little bejeweled bugs creeping up her back. How evil-queen of her!

'I LITERALLY CAN'T' SLEEVES

Fashion's cool girls used to wear their coats as capes, but lately stars like Hailey Baldwin and Kendall Jenner are updating the look by letting their sleeves slink down to their elbows. Does everyone have really sensitive shoulders? Or is there a sleeves allergy in Hollywood that everyone else is immune to?

LACE LEGGINGS

Ultra-feminine fabric in an ultra-snug silhouette makes for a head-turning pair of tights, like Hailee Steinfeld’s.

THIGH WINDOW DRESSES

Don’t want to wear a mini but think that a midi is just too much coverage? Michelle Monaghan’s peek-a-boo dress is the best of both worlds.

PATCHWORK UTILITY PANTS

Emly Ratajkowski can manage to make slouchy cargos look sexy, but we're still pretty stuck on the pants' pattern: It’s a combo of “groovy” and “grunge.”

CROPPED CHAMBRAY PEPLUMS

Peplums are designed to emphasize the smallest part of your waist. In the case of Emma Roberts’ crop top version, the waist is indeed emphasized, but that’s because there’s a frilly ruffle right on it.

PAINTED MOTOS

Some stars really love to wear other stars’ faces on their clothes, but leave it to Kim Kardashian (who, let’s not forget, published a book of selfies) to wear herself on her leather jacket.

MATCHING JUMPSUIT AND BLAZER

Average ladies would’ve paired this metallic jumpsuit with a black blazer, or this metallic blazer with a black jumpsuit, but Darby Stanchfield chooses to double the impact with an all-shine, all-the-time ensemble.

PATCHWORK PLACKETS

Retro patchwork skirts are having a moment, and Karrueche Tran has honed in on a micro-trend that packs a totally macro punch.

PEARL TRIMMING

Of course Broadway vet Kristen Chenoweth found an outfit that maximizes the impact of jazz hands and fancy footwork— it’s like an ultra-glam wearable maraca!

ALL-OVER MACRAME

This is a picture of Allison Williams in a cozy dress. Yes, we said “cozy.” It does look a wee bit like a crochet 1970s blanket, right? A cozy, body-con blanket.

HAIRY BLAZERS

Are you prepared to open up your work wardrobe to the free-flowing vibe fringe gives off? Megan Fox is counting on it. (We don't know how your boss feels.)

THE CASUAL BEEHIVE UPDO

For most gals, getting coffee with friends isn't an occasion for a major throwback hair style. But most gals aren’t pop stars like Lana Del Rey, who once sang in a song called “Summertime Sadness:” “Done my hair up real big, beauty-queen-style / High heels off, I’m feeling alive.” (That's how we feel about unlimited mimosas, too.)

MESH SKIRTS

We didn’t think there would be a way to make a totally see-through skirt even more scandalous, but Kylie Jenner opted to wear a nude bodysuit under hers, giving the illusion of, well, nudity!

EMBELLISHED PETER PAN COLLARS

Is a bauble-bedecked detail like this exactly the little bit of pixie dust Olivia Munn and Kaitlin Doubleday need to spice up their LBDs?

ALL OVER FARMER VIBES

Pick just one element of this look (overalls, hat or leather-and-denim-bag), and it's trendy and wearable. But does combining all three, like Olivia Wilde opted to, feel a little too country girl?

STRUCTURED BELL SLEEVES

The elbow peplum strikes again, this time on Jamie Chung. And these sleeves look almost sturdy enough to actually ring.

SCORTCHED EDGES

Bella Hadid's Moschino mini combines luxe fabric with purposely ragged details. File this under “Things Not to Wear to Brunch with Grandma Because She Wouldn't Understand.”

LOWER-WAIST CUTOUTS

Many ladies go to great lengths to cover the area that Alessandra Ambrosio is showcasing, and since we so rarely see that section of skin in all its glory we have to applaud the model for taking the risk.

THE 3-D FLORAL BOLERO

We never really considered retro bathing caps to be fashion inspo, but if anyone were able to put the idea in her heads it would indeed be Gwen Stefani.

THE HIGH-LOW PONCHO

FURRY INSETS

LAMÉ FOR DAY

STATEMENT HEADBANDS

PURPOSELY DIRTY JEANS

PURSE NECKLACES
