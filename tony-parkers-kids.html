Who Are Tony Parker’s Kids? 

Tony Parker has two sons, Liam Parker and Josh Parker. Among the two, Josh is the eldest son, born in April 2014, while Liam is the youngest one, born in July 2016. 

Both of his sons were born in San Antonio, Texas.

“I am glad to be a father. My wife is pregnant, and our first son Josh will have a little brother. My son Liam and his mommy are doing well.” – Tony Parker said after his second child’s birth.

Who Is His Kids’ Baby Momma?

Tony Parker’s baby momma is the French journalist Axelle Francine. The duo met back in 2011 and later married her in 2014. 

They were deeply in love with each other and tied the knot on August 2, 2014, in San Antonio, Texas. In fact, their pre-wedding reception was held at Cowboy Dancehall. 

However, on August 3, 2020, Tony tweeted that his and Axelle’s relationship was ending. After years of knowing and being there for each other, the duo parted ways and filed for a divorce. 

“After nine years together, during which we shared a lot of happiness and raised two wonderful children, Axelle and I have decided to end our union. This separation will be guided by complete mutual respect and the preservation of the well-being of our two sons.” – Tony Parker.
