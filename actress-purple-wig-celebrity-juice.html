TV star looks unrecognisable as she dresses up as Katy Perry

August 27, 2024
blog

EMILY Atack looked unrecognisable as she transformed into Katy Perry this evening.

The 32-year-old looked amazing wearing a purple wig and mini dress – taking inspiration from the singer's California Gurls video, as she filmed Celebrity Juice.

She teamed the ice cream themed dress with a pair of pink platform heels as she posed up a storm in her dressing room.

Emily, who is now a team caption on the show, later shared a clip with the host Keith Lemon once they had wrapped filming.

She took over from Holly Willoughby and now works opposite Laura Whitmore, who is the other team captain.

Laura appeared to be dressed up as Kylie Minogue from her Can’t Get You Out of my Head video, completely with white jumpsuit.

Speaking previously about landing the gig, Emily said: “I would say Celebrity Juice is a real pinnacle moment for me, because it's one of the bigger shows on television. Stepping into shoes like Holly Willoughby's is the most ridiculous and daunting path as a telly person.

"It's like, 'How on earth will I fill shoes like that?!' But I'm just so humbled and flattered, and I'm glad to be doing the show with Laura, she's my really good friend."

Filming for the next series of Celeb Juice started at the end of last month.
