HAVIG TOO MUCH FUN?

The news comes after Jeopardy! host Ken Jennings poked fun at a contestant over his plans for his game show winnings. 

It kicked off after Ken teased the audience: "Many Jeopardy! contestants come on the show with a plan on what they would do with their winnings but Navid Mehrjou has one I've never heard before..." 

Contestant Navid Mehrjou then shared that he planned on enjoying a murder mystery cruise if he won.

Ken asked: "Is there a specific type of murder mystery cruise?" 

Navid answered: "The Golden Girls had an episode back in the day where the ladies went to a hotel, and it was a murder mystery...and it was amazing.

"And that has made me want to take a murder mystery cruise."

The audience laughed when Ken joked: "The Golden Girls have determined the course of your life, perhaps?"

Navid added: "In this endeavor and many others."

The contestant turned away as the audience continued laughing.

Ken will be hosting the ABC game show until January.

She asked: "These stars are ready for prime time, are you?

"Watch your favorite celebrities put their brains and buzzer to the test on Celebrity Jeopardy!"
