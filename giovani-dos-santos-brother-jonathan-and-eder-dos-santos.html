Giovani Dos Santos has two brother, Jonathan and Éder Dos Santos, who are also soccer players.

Giovani dos Santos Ramiez, born on May 11, 1989, is a Mexican professional soccer player known for versatility.

Dos Santos has achieved notable success in his career, including winning an Olympic gold medal.

The player throughout his career has played as an attacking midfielder, winger, and secondary striker.

Giovani made his first professional debut for Barcelona’s senior squad for their pre-season tour against AGF Aarhus on July 29, 2006.

His performance impressed the club, and on November 28, 2006, Dos Santos was included in Barcelona’s 23-man squad at FIFA Club World Cup in Japan.

Indeed, on May 17, 2008, Giovani dos Santos had an impressive performance for Barcelona as he scored a hat-trick in a match against Real Murcia, helping his team secure a 5-3 victory.

It was also Dos Santos’ last appearance for Barcelona before he moved to join Tottenham Hotspur.

Finally, his hat trick marked a memorable end to his time with Barcelona and showcased his talent and potential as a young player.

Giovani Dos Santos Brother: Jonathan And Eder dos Santos

Three siblings were born in Mexico to their parents, Geraldo Francisco and Liliana Ramirez.

Giovani Dos Santos Brother, Eder dos Santos, played for America’s reserves team but retired in 2009. 

Eder’s career may not have reached the same heights as his brothers. However, he played a role in soccer and passed on his passion for the sport to his brothers.

After retiring from his soccer career, Eder hasn’t been seen on the soccer field. Instead, he works in a marketing agency called “ADDICTIVE.”

Defensive Midfielder Jonathan dos Santos

Jonathan dos Santos Ramirez, younger brother of Giovani, is also a soccer player who plays as a defensive midfielder for the Liga MX club America.

Jonathan dos Santos, born on April 26, 1990, is the youngest of three children in his family.

As inspired by his older brother’s success, he made his own professional soccer journey and joined F.C. Barcelona B in 2009.

It was a significant step in his career as he sought to make a name for himself in the world of soccer.

Moreover, Dos Santos has also played for the Mexican national team and has also stints with various clubs, including Barcelona B, Villarreal, and L.A. Galaxy.

His role as a defensive midfielder allows him to contribute to his team’s defensive solidity while also providing support in the attacking phase of the game.

The Santos Brothers Are Son Of Former Soccer Player Zizinho

Giovani Dos Santos is the son of former soccer player Geraldo Francisco dos Santos, popularly known as Zizinho.

He was a Brazilian soccer player who played a significant time of his career in Mexican soccer.

In addition, he was one of the skilled midfielders, particularly for Club America.

Zizinho’s contributions to the sport and his performances on the field earned him recognition and admiration among fans and fellow players.

Unfortunately, he passed away on July 29, 2021, leaving behind a legacy in the world of soccer.
