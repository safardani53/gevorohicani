The best family cars for any budget in 2019 - from affordable hatchbacks to luxury SUVs

April 20, 2024
blog

CHOOSING a car to meet the needs of all members of the family can be tough.

Safety, efficiency, price and even comfort are all important factors to weigh up when making your decision.

To make your job easier, a breakdown by car specialists Which? has revealed the best family cars currently on the market.

Each vehicle was tested by driving experts to find out whether they are reliable, durable and can keep your loved ones safe.

From affordable hatchbacks to comfortable SUVs, these are the best motors for your family.

Ford Focus - from £16,676

The Ford Focus is one of the most popular motors for UK drivers and it's easy to see why.

It comes with plenty of room for the family with five seats, a decent boot and plenty of leg room.

The Focus also scored an impressive five-star NCAP rating as it includes an autonomous emergency breaking system and evasive steering technology.

Honda Civic - from £17,365

The Honda Civic has always been an attractive option for families looking for a reliable motor which doesn't break the bank.

The family car is easy to drive, comfortable and offers great value for money.

Owners looking to pack up the car and head off for the weekend will enjoy its spacious boot and in-built sat nav.

Volkswagen Golf - from £20,290

Another popular choice with Brits, the Volkswagen Golf is the perfect car for families who are always on the go.

The Golf is comfortable and great fun to drive, whether you're in the city or on the motorway.

Experts have praised the motor for its ability to cope in a range of conditions.

Volvo XC40 - £27,065

The XC40 has quickly become a favoured option for young families in the market for a SUV.

Despite being smaller than your average SUV, it still has a spacious interior and a large boot.

The car also scored five-stars in the Euro NCAP crash test, with a 87 per cent child occupant protection rating.

BMW X3 - from £37,337

Some could be put off by its price tag, but the BMW X3 will certainly give you value for your money.

The spacious family SUV is ideal for families who want to travel in style, thanks to its roomy interior and leather seats.

It is even capable of towing up to 2.4 tonnes if you're looking to take the boat or caravan on holiday.

Volvo V60 - from £34,557

Like the XC40, the Volvo V60 is an excellent choice if you're in the market for a spacious vehicle.

Drivers will enjoy getting behind the wheel as it has a very quiet cabin and comfortable seats.

It even comes with a Pilot Assist driving system, which helps keep your car within its own lane and maintain a safe distance from vehicles in front of you.

BMW 6 Series Gran Turismo - from £37,773

It may look too sporty to be a family wagon, but the BMW 6 Series Gran Turismo is more than it appears.

The classy vehicle has a powerful engine, stylish interior and a roomy boot.

The Gran Turismo is also flush with useful technology, including an all-around parking sensors, rear-view camera and automatic lights.
