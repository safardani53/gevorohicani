Scarlett Johansson and Colin Jost got married in 2020 and welcomed their son in 2021. See best pics together here.

Colin Jost and Scarlett Johansson may have one of the most quintessential Hollywood stories! The pair met each other back in 2010 when Scarlett was hosting ‘Saturday Night Live,’ where Colin was a writer. The pair started off as friends and continued to support each other in their career endeavors — Colin became a head writer at ‘SNL’ and Scarlett became one of Marvel’s biggest and brightest stars! The two started dating in 2017 and their relationship flourished into something so much more. In May 2019, Colin popped the question to Scarlett, proposing with an 11-carat brown diamond — and she said yes! In August 2021, the couple expanded their family when they welcomed their first child together, a son named Cosmo. Over the years, the happy couple has often made red carpet appearances together, and Colin was right by Scarlett’s side during the 2019-2020 awards season following the actress’ success with film ‘Jojo Rabbit’ and ‘Marriage Story.’ To see more photos of the couple through the years, check out the gallery!
