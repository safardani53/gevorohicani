SHE'S the only Kardashian-Jenner sister who hasn't had a baby yet, and it doesn't look like Kendall Jenner is going to be starting a family any time soon.

Kendall, 24, recently enjoyed a vacation in Palm Springs, California, with her famous siblings and gave fans a glimpse on social media.

Taking to Snapchat, the brunette model posted a hilarious video of herself taking a dip in the swimming pool as her nieces and nephews splashed around in the background.

Smiling for the camera whilst wearing a green bikini and black sunglasses, Kendall gave a thumbs up and captioned the clip: "Still no kids."

Kendall is the last non-parent Kardashian-Jenner sister who hasn't started a family yet.

Her 22-year-old sister, Kylie, is already a mom to two-year-old daughter Stormi, while her big sister, Khloé Kardashian, has a daughter named True - who will turn two in April.

Meanwhile, fellow big sisters Kim and Kourtney have multiple children.

Kim, 39, is mom to North West, who will turn seven this year, five-year-old Saint West, two-year-old Chicago and baby Psalm, who will turn one in May.

Kourtney, 40, has three children with her ex partner Scott Disick, and they are 10-year-old Mason, seven-year-old Penelope, and Reign, five.

However, it seems Kendall hasn't ruled out becoming a mom and in a March 2018 interview with Vogue, she shared that she wants to become a parent, but when she's older.

"I am ready to wait," she said at the time. "I want to have kids, but at, like, 28 or 29."
