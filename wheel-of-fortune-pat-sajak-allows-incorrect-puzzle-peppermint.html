Wheel of Fortune fans cry foul after Pat Sajak allows player to 'break the rules' and solve puzzle '

August 06, 2024
blog

WHEEL of Fortune's Pat Sajak has allowed a contestant's response even though she may have broken the rules of the game by adding a word to her answer.

A Twitter storm brewed after Corrine answered the crossword puzzle on Wednesday's episode and the host let it slide.

On December 6th's Secret Santa week show, the crossword puzzle's prompt was "Peppermint," as displayed under the letter board.

Corrine guessed the last of the vowels, leaving only two letters blank in the four-word puzzle.

"I'll solve," she grinned and told Pat, 75.

The host said before she did so: "Say everything, don't add anything. Go ahead."

Corrine then guessed: "Peppermint - sprinkles, oil, toffee, tea."

Pat deemed it correct: "Yes, you got it - there you go."

"Anyway, you've got $1,150 dollars and I'm going to sit down and take a break," Pat said cutting to the commercial.

Corrine's score went to $1.15K and she wound up winning the episode with $26K total.

'HOW WAS THAT ALLOWED?'

Wheel of Fortune viewers flooded Twitter and collectively spun out, arguing that Corrine did add something to her answer despite Pat's plain instruction.

She added "Peppermint," the prompt below the board, then said for the four words actually on the board.

"Wait a min....... that counted?" one viewer tweeted at the game show in astonishment.

Another wrote: "I’m confused. The contestant said 'peppermint' before solving the puzzle and they gave her credit. With the Crossword category, you’re not supposed to add anything."

"Woah woah woah! I object," wrote a third as a fourth agreed: "Technically she added peppermint which isn’t on the board."

A fifth slammed: "My whole family and my friends are wondering why Corinne got credit for solving the crossword puzzle--where she can't add anything--and she added the word Peppermint!"

"The word 'peppermint' wasn't in the puzzle! It was the clue! Maggie [Sajak] can you explain this?"

A sixth agreed: "Wheel of Fortune, How come the lady won the money tonight when she said peppermint when she said the words to the crossword?"

While a seventh rang in: "Whoa Wheel of Fortune Changed the rules again?!?  She added Peppermint to her answer. You guys need to give up the crossword for real."

One more of dozens asked: "I’m confused as to how that’s okay? I get that it's in the title but you’re so strict with the 'and' and you always say don’t add anything …. Soooooo????"

"I think Pat hesitated if they can accept that or not," yet one more couldn't ignore as someone replied to them: "They’re so strict about adding anything! I’m surprised he let this go."

'DEPRESSING TO THINK ABOUT'

Pat's 40-year partner in letter plucking, Vanna revealed her thoughts on being replaced to People - and she isn't planning on it.

"I think when Merv Griffin chose both of us, 40 and 41 years ago, he saw something between us — a brother-sister type relationship.

"And I think that is kind of what it is. He saw that we would be able to get along, and we do. We are like a brother and sister team."

On retirement, she added: "I don't even want to think about that. I mean, we're a team.

That's depressing. I don't even want to think about it."

"I just visualize us just being there. I can't think beyond that."
