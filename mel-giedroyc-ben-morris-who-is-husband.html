How old is Mel Giedroyc?

Mel Giedroyc is an English actress, comedian, and television presenter.

Born in Epsom, Surrey on June 5, 1968, to parents Michal and Rosemary, at the time of writing, Mel Giedroyc is 55 years old.

The comedian is the youngest of four, she has two sisters - Coky Giedroyc and Kasia Param and one brother, Miko Giedroyc.

Prior to finding success, she graduated from the Trinity College in Cambridge with a degree in French and Italian.

Away from her family life, Mel is mostly known for her comedy and presenting work with Sue Perkins, which has included co-hosting Light Lunch for Channel 4, The Great British Bake Off, and the chat show Mel and Sue for ITV.

The popular presenter and comedian is also part of the Children In Need hosting team - a role she first took on in 2017 and continues to hold to this day.

Who is Mel Giedroyc's husband Ben Morris?

As well as finding career success, Mel has also been lucky enough to find love.

She is married to her husband Ben Morris, a television director and teacher at the world-leading drama school LAMDA.

The happy couple tied the knot after previously meeting at a dinner party two years prior.

Other notable titles Mel has appeared in include The Generation Game, numerous commentary roles for the Eurovision Song Contest, Strictly Come Dancing Christmas Special, Hitmen, and Mel Giedroyc: Unforgivable.

How many children do Mel Giedroyc and Ben Morris have?
