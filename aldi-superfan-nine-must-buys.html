Im an Aldi superfan - nine must-buys starting at $2 and one you should think twice about because

July 14, 2024
blog

ALDI has tons of great finds and is typically priced lower than most.

Plus, Aldi brands like Earth Grown and Simply Nature tend to be better for you and a better deal.

At least according to TikToker Katie Kelly.

Katie is a lifestyle and food blogger with over 138,000 followers on TikTok.

She has tons of videos sharing her favorite finds from Aldi and personal recipes to try.

Katie, who goes by bitsbitesblog on TikTok, recently posted a video revealing the nine products you must buy at Aldi and one to be cautious of.

1. Rosemary and Feta Krinkle Cut Kettle Chips

For only $1.99, the Journey To…Rosemary and Feta Krinkle Cut Kettle Chips are definitely worth the purchase.

The eight-ounce bag is gluten-free and has only 150 calories per serving size.

2. Almond Butter Filled Pretzels

These Almond Butter Filled Pretzels from Simply Nature are only $1.99.

Most read in Money

The bag comes with 15 ounces of perfectly salted pretzel bites and contains no GMO.

3. Earth Grown Vegan Vegetable Pot Stickers

The pot stickers are only $5.49 and are quickly becoming a fan favorite.

The gluten-free item comes with dipping sauces and is ready in just eight to 12 minutes.

4. Appetitos Mini Spring Rolls

These mini spring rolls are only $4.99 and make for an easy meal.

Katie suggests popping them in the air fryer for a nice crisp.

For an eight-ounce box, shoppers can choose from the Buffalo Chicken flavor or Philly Cheesesteak flavor.

5. Nature’s Nector Sparkling Natural Soda

This is the product that shoppers should think twice about.

Even though the TikToker says they taste great, the soda contains 20 grams of sugar.

At her store, the sodas were on clearance for $2.69.

6. Simply Nature Seaweed Snacks

These seaweed snacks were $1.89.

They contain no GMO and no preservatives.

The ingredients are pretty clean: seaweed, non-GMO corn oil, non-GMO expeller pressed sesame oil, non-GMO expeller pressed perilla oil, and baked salt.

These are an Aldi Find meaning they will only be in store for a limited time.

7. Journey To... Spanakopita

The Journey To… Spanakopita is $4.49 and is the perfect appetizer.

Katie said this is another great item to put in the air fryer for quick and simple cooking.

8. Earth Grown Korean BBQ Vegan Dumpling

The Earth Grown Korean BBQ Vegan Dumplings are just $3.99 and come with five pieces.

The brand also makes a Tofu and Vegetable version as well as a Thai Basil flavor.

9. Hot Honey Pepperoni Pizza pretzel-style crust

The Mama Cozzi Hot Honey Pepperoni Pizza is only $5.49.

It's a re-released pizza and is available in the Hot Honey pepperoni and BBQ chicken varieties. 

It's the perfect mix of sweet and salty and surprises you with the pretzel crust.

10. Earth Grown Vegan ice cream

Vegan ice cream may sound like a bad idea, but Katie swears by it.

This seasonal item is $3.49 on Instacart and comes in three flavors: Chocolate, Mocha Fudge, or Vanilla.

Tips to save money on groceries

Beyond sticking to a list and planning your meals in advance to avoid overspending or wasting food, there are plenty of ways you can shave extra dollars off your grocery bill.  

Aldi's weekly ad circular includes information on deals, and you can find that online and in stores.

In addition to Aldi's deals, you can get extra savings by taking advantage of cashback and couponing apps that accept Aldi receipts.
