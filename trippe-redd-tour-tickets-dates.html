Trippie Redd's Take Me Away tour 2023: How can I get tickets?

August 16, 2024
blog

RAPPER Trippie Redd announced he was going on tour in support of his album, A Love Letter To You 5.

With Trippie Redd preparing to embark on his summer 2023 tour, fans are eager to know how to get their hands on tickets.

When is Trippie Redd going on tour?

On June 28, 2023, Ohio rapper Trippie Redd announced he'd be embarking on a 24-date Take Me Away tour.

The tour, starting on August 31, is meant to promote his forthcoming album, A Letter To You 5.

Though Redd has yet to announce when his album will hit the shelves, in February 2023, the rapper said it all comes down to getting the perfect cover art to go with his music.

Redd told Rolling Stone: "I’m trying to come up with the cover art and add a few songs for that record. So, I wanted to do this new one to do a Lil Wayne, Sorry for the Wait, type of deal."

What opening acts will be on the Take Me Away tour?

Trippie Redd will be embarking on his tour with an array of artists opening for him.

These artists include Jean Dawson, D Savage, EKKSTACY, and K Suave.

Additionally, Chicago rapper Lucki is named as one of the main openers on the Take Me Away tour.

Trippie Redd and Lucki have a song together called DIE DIE which was released on January 20, 2023.

How can I get tickets to Trippie Redd's tour?

Tickets to Trippie Redd's Take Me Away tour can be bought via Ticketmaster.

The presale begins Thursday, June 29, at 10am local time.

Additionally, the general sale is set to begin on Friday, June 30, at 11am local time.
