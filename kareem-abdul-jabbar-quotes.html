He was also privileged as one of the 50 Greatest Players in NBA History. His retirement was done in 1989 when he was 42 years old. At that time, he was the NBA’s all-time leader. In 2016, he was rewarded with the Presidential Medal of Freedom by President of U.S. Barack Obama.
