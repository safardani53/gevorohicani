33rd Annual Footwear News Achievement Awards 2019 Pics Hollywood Life

July 31, 2024
blog

The best dressed stars came out to support the hottest names in shoe design at the 2019 Footwear News Achievement Awards on December 3, 2019. At the 33rd Annual FNAAs, The Dr. Martens 1460 Boot received FN’s highest honor as “Shoe of the Year,” while Amina Muaddi was honored with “Designer of the Year.” Other honorees included Kerby Jean-Raymond (Person of the Year), Tommy Hilfiger (Collaborator of the Year), J Balvin (Style Influencer of the Year), Jeffrey Kalinsky, Steve Madden and Sandra Choi (Hall of Fame), Donna Karan and Kenneth Cole (Icon Awards for Social Impact), Bob Campbell (Lifetime Achievement Award), VF Corporation (Company of the Year), DICK’S Sporting Goods (Retailer of the Year), Puma and Rothy’s (Brands of the Year), Midnight 00 and Paris Texas (Emerging Talent Awards). Celebrities flooded the IAC building for the fete, including Adriana Lima, who presented to PUMA; Christie Brinkley, who was there to support Kenneth Cole; and Kat Graham, who gave a gorgeous speech for Midnight 00’s founder, Ada Kokosar.
