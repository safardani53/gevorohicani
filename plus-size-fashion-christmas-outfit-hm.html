A WOMAN has found the perfect plus size Christmas outfit from H&M that is both comfortable and flattering.

Laura Adlington shared her festive find on TikTok, where she often shares plus size fashion inspiration.

In one video, Laura shared a gorgeous Christmas look, perfect for spending time with your family on Christmas Day.

She started her look off with an incredibly flattering off the shoulder jumper, which is on sale at H&M for £21.99.

Laura got the jumper in a size 26 and it had a slightly oversized look, meaning you can eat and drink plenty on Christmas Day without having to worry.

The fashion pro paired the jumper with a striking sparkly silver skirt wraparound skirt from Never Fully Dressed.

Laura tucked her jumper into the skirt before tying it at the side to create a fun but comfortable looking ensemble.

As the skirt is wraparound it's perfect for adjusting after you've eaten Christmas dinner, so you can stay comfortable for the whole day.

To accessorise her look, Laura chose a pink clutch bag from Mint Velvet that was covered in fluff, perfect for jazzing up any outfit.

Then she added some simple silver open-toed heels from Next to give the outfit an air of elegance.
