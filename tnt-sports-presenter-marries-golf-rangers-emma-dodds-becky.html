TNT Sports presenter marries golf star as Rangers heroes congratulate happy couple

April 20, 2024
blog

SCOTS telly presenter Emma Dodds is celebrating after getting married to long term partner Becky Morgan.

The TNT Sports host tied the knot with Welsh golf star Becky in a stunning Loch Lomond ceremony.

Emma posted a series of pictures from the big occasion, captioned on social media "best day ever."

The Rangers TV favourite can be seen looking incredible in her white dress on the idyllic loch-side setting.

Emma and Becky got engaged over three years ago.

Posting to Instagram in September 2020, Dodds said: "Some personal news.

"After a few months to enjoy this privately with friends and family, I'm beyond thrilled to say @bjmgolf said yes!"

And almost three years on, the happy couple said 'I do' on Saturday - explaining exactly why Emma wasn't hosting Rangers' cup clash with Morton on Viaplay!

While it's Emma who's the more familiar face to Scottish sports fans, Becky has a hugely successful pro golf career behind her.

A former winner on the Ladies European Tour, Morgan has a top-10 Women's PGA Championship finish to her name as well as a top-15 placing at the Women's British Open.

Several Rangers heroes and broadcasting colleagues took to social media to congratulate the newly weds on their big day.

Kevin Thomson posted: "Congrats Emma."

Rangers legend Derek Parlane, former Scotland rugby ace John Barclay and music group Saint Phnx were among the others to pass on their well wishes.
