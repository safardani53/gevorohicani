Kim Kardashian, Beyonce, Lizzo, Kylie Jenner and Drake show off their GRILLZ for a blinged-out smile

September 02, 2024
blog

KIM Kardashian is the latest celebrity showing off her grillz, following in the footsteps of stars like Beyonce, Drake, and her sister Kylie Jenner.

The dental jewelry, which is worn over the teeth, has become a statement piece for many over the years.

Kim, 40, showed off her sparkling set on Wednesday, posting a close-up of her mouth on Instagram.

The mother of four, who recently filed for divorce from husband Kanye West, pulled down on her bottom lip to show off the jewelry on her bottom row of teeth.

She captioned the shot, detailing the precious piece: “new opal birthstone grill alert” 

From Gabby Elan Jewelry, the grillz featured opals shaped to fit over each individual tooth, with what appears to be diamond studs lining the permiter of each, as well as acting as the connector between them all.

The jewelry company shared video of their creation, showing that from different angles and distances, fans can see many different colors within the gemstones.

“New Opal set for @kimkardashian,” they captioned the clip.

Kim also famously showed off her blinged-out teeth, as well as her massive engagement ring, in an Instagram photo just days before she was robbed in Paris.

This comes as sources told Heat magazine that Drake, 34, has been messaging Kim, saying he started to do so the “moment it became clear that [her] divorce was inevitable.”

Drake has been known to rock grillz in his teeth over the years and wore them in the music video for his hit song In My Feelings in 2018.

The rapper dropped $14,000 for the platinum and 18-karat gold dental jewelry created by Ben Baller.

Kim’s sister Kylie has also rocked the look on quite a few occasions.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder, 23, started showing off grillz since the early 2010s, as she shared a selfie of her at In-N-Out wearing a gold piece in 2013.

She showed up to the American Music Awards in 2014 wearing a low-cut, deep-red satin dress and black-and-teal hair, but it was the metal on her teeth that really made a statement.

During her relationship with ex Tyga, Kylie was gifted an 18-carat diamond-encrusted grill by her boyfriend for Valentine’s Day.

Wearing a pink wig and a black beanie hat, the reality star showed off the new mouthpiece, which was designed by Johnny Dang aka TV Johnny and cost $15,000.

Award shows have been a go-to place for celebrities to show off their grillz, and Lizzo did just that at the 2021 Grammys on Sunday.

While posing on the red carpet in a green, shimmering, strapless Balmain dress, the 32-year-old singer pulled open the sides of her mouth with both of her pinky fingers to show off the sparkling jewelry capping her teeth.

Personalized grillz are what queen bey has been about, and the record-breaking Grammy winner wore specialized ones for her Ivy Park collaboration with Adidas last year.
