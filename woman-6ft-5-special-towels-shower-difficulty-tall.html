Im 6ft 5 and struggle with some of the easiest things in life - I even have to use special towels

May 10, 2024
blog

SHE was 4ft 11 by the time she was 10 years old, and 5ft 7 by 13.

Now 25, Ellie measures 6ft 5 when she's not wearing high heels, and regularly posts on TikTok about the everyday difficulties of being a tall girl.

She recently shared a clip in which she bemoaned the size of regular bath towels.

As she held it up against herself, it managed to wrap around her due to her lithe figure, but didn't give as much "coverage" as she wanted.

"Tell me why I did not know that bath sheets exist?" she said.

"So this is a bath sheet, I found it at Target and it is like the size of a beach towel."

Demonstrating once again by holding the towel up to her body, she continued: "It has so much coverage, and I've just been walking around in this tiny thing my whole life.

"So I will be switching exclusively to these.

"And if you're tall and you didn't know this existed, you're welcome."

"Tall people problems? Bath towels. It might sound silly to some but I'm so glad I found these," Ellie captioned her video.
