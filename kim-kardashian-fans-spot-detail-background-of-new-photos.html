KIM Kardashian fans have spotted a "bizarre" detail in the background of her new glam photos.

Kim posted a picture of her in her beautiful silver dress before the Kardashians' premiere on Instagram.

"THE KARDASHIANS PREMIERE! We premiered two weeks ago and I’m blown away that we had the highest premiere in HULU History! Beyond grateful when we heard that news!!" she captioned the post.

"Also, I felt so honored to wear a special dress to the special night! Manfred Mugler made this dress for me last year before he passed away and I just felt the magic in him when I wore it!," Kim finished.

The mirror picture was then followed by a bunch of photos of her and her boyfriend, Pete Davidson, at the premiere.

PHOTOSHOP FAILS

In the same photo, the 41-year-old was accused of photoshopping herself to have a skinnier waist and bigger hips.

Eagle-eyed followers claimed they'd spotted a tell-tale sign that she had edited the shot, pointing out what appeared to be the "warped" doorframe behind her

"Immediately in the first pic I noticed the warp on the door behind her, on the left side of the pic where she obviously photoshopped her waist to high heaven."

Another added: "She’s obviously very skinny right now but that waist is ridiculous lol. Incredibly fake.

A third posted: "We know you’re skinny! You’re skinny enough. Stop with the photoshopping. This looks ridiculous."

BELLY BUTTON BE GONE

However, this is not the first time the reality star has been called out for touching up her photos.

Earlier this week, Kardashian fans claimed she had photoshopped her waist so much while posing in a crop top that her belly button had disappeared.

Kim posed in a SKIMS sports bra and underwear set with her hands through her hair, as she stood by a pool.

She placed sweatpants over her SKIMS and captioned the photo, "Sunday in my @skims."

But fans weren't there for her having fun in the sun, pointing out that she clearly altered the pics.

"Her waist is most definitely photoshopped," one fan on Reddit said.

And many other fans claimed she photoshopped her belly button out as it was present in one photo but not another.
