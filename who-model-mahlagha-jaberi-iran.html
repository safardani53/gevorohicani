Who is Iranian model Mahlagha Jaberi?

Mahlagha Jaberi is a popular model who was born on June 17, 1989, in Isfahan, Iran.

After moving to the United States, she began working in the fashion industry and has since amassed a large presence on social media.

Her portfolio includes work with Styé Cruze magazine, among others.

Jaberi often posts about her life on Instagram to over 4.2million followers.

What did Mahlagha Jaberi wear to the 2023 Cannes Film Festival?

Jaberi made a political statement at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival in an attempt to raise awareness about a series of executions happening in Iran.

The dress, which was designed by Jila Saber, was all black and resembled a noose.

It also had to words "stop executions" written on the back.

"We wanted to make a fashion statement to observe the glamour of Cannes, but more importantly, to bring media attention to the wrongful executions of Iranian people," Jaberi said of the dress in an Instagram post.
