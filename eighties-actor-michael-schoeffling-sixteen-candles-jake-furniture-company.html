Samantha craved the attention of handsome school jock Jake, with his brunette locks and stylish dress sense.

The pair finally got together in the movie's closing scenes, much to the delight of fans.

Yet now he has completely changed career path.

Michael, who now wears glasses and has a close-crop haircut, lives in Pennsylvania with wife Valerie.

The dad then started a carpentry and furniture business according to complex.com.

He is CEO of the company which focusses on hand-crafted items, yet little else is known about his business.
