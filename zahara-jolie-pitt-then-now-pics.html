Happy birthday, Zahara Jolie-Pitt! Celebrate Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt’s daughter’s 19th birthday with her best photos.

From baby girl to full-fledged fashionista! Zahara Jolie-Pitt is the beloved daughter of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt. Only six months old when Angelina adopted her from an orphanage in Ethiopia in July of 2005, Brad and Angelina were just one month shy of their first anniversary at the time. Afterward. Brad went on to adopt Zahara and Maddox (whom Angelina had adopted from Cambodia in 2002) in 2006.

Zahara is now the older sister of three other siblings — Shiloh, Vivienne and Knox — and is also the little sister of Pax, whom Angelina and Brad adopted in 2007. She is incredibly close with her family, and has been spotted on many outings with her siblings and parents throughout the years, caught out shopping or hitting red carpets with mom.

In summer 2022, Zahara embarked on her college journey at Spelman College in Atlanta. Mom was so proud, celebrating her acceptance in an Aug. Instagram. She helped settle her daughter when she moved and visited again for Homecoming weekend in Oct. 2022.
