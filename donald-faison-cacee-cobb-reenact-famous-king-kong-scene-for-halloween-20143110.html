Donald Faison, CaCee Cobb Reenact Famous King Kong Scene for Halloween

May 29, 2024
blog

Jessica's BFF is in trouble! CaCee Cobb and Donald Faison poked fun of King Kong's most iconic scene for Halloween on Thursday, Oct. 30. The Scrubs alum and Cobb, who is expecting baby No. 2, went as King Kong and the damsel in distress Ann Darrow from the 1933 classic.

"You know what they say about big hands," Cobb, 36, jokingly captioned the Instagram picture. Cobb donned a light yellow dress and kept her blonde locks down, reminiscent of Fay Wray's portrayal of actress Darrow in the fantasy adventure.

Faison, 40, kept hidden in the gorilla suit as he wrapped an oversized hand around his wife's waist. In the original film, the giant ape grabs a screaming Darrow and climbs New York's Empire State Building while being attacked by airplanes.

The couple wore the hilarious getup to attend Kate Hudson's annual star-studded Halloween bash in L.A. The parents have yet to reveal their son Rocco's costume, but Cobb did post a snapshot of his outfit last year earlier in the day.

"#tbt Me and Darth Roc. Halloween 2013," she captioned the shot of her little man, now 14 months.
