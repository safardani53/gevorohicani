Huge US drama kicking off on Channel 5 tonight after shock axe

A HUGE US drama is launching on Channel 5 tonight following its shock axe earlier this year.

Drama series Yellowstone, which stars Hollywood actor Kevin Costner, is making is UK television debut.

The series, which was created by Taylor Sheridan and John Linson, first aired on Paramount.

Set in Montana, the plot revolves around a Dutton family ranch and the bordering Broken Rock Indian Reservation, national park, and developers.

Throughout the series, the Dutton family and their employees face trouble from obstacles like land developers and the bordering Native reservations and national parks.

Series one of Yellowstone kicks off on Channel 5 tonight, Friday, at 9pm.
