STACEY Solomon has wowed fans with her stunning walk-in wardrobe with its glamorous dressing table and light-up storage.

Taking to her Instagram stories to share a sweet video of son Rex playing in the wardrobe, fans were immediately drawn to its beautiful decor.

Stacey, 31, shared how she was inundated with messages after posting the clip of her youngest son trying to hide behind some pampas grass.

"Playing hide and seek in the wardrobe this morning," she captioned the video of two-year-old causing havoc by getting in her closet.

"He kills me, I've never seen someone run so fast in such a small space," she went on alongside a sweet snap of her tot.

The Loose Women panellist added that "so many" of her fans had asked for details of her wardrobe thanks to its stunning decor.

As well as the decorative grass, Stacey had used a dark beige paint on the walls to add a touch of glamour to the small space.

White wardrobes line up along both the walls, with light-up shelving units inside to house all her clothes and bags and make them easy to see.

The room also features an elegant dressing table, fit with plenty of drawers and storage space, topped with more pampas grass and candles.

Soon-to-be mother-of-four Stacey also teased her autumnal set-up for the top of the table, including a small pumpkin trinket and a decorative knome.

Perfect for doing her makeup, the spot also has a black mirror and teddy-bear style chair with opulent gold-painted legs.

The room is all tied together by a stunning plush cream carpet, which the singing star previously dubbed "impractical" due to her three children.

"It's the only piece of carpet we will ever have in the whole house with four kids, two dogs and a cat," she told fans as she hoovered the room.

"So I went with the most unmanageable and impractical fluffy cream carpet I could," she concluded, showing off the entire room.

'MORE EXCITING THAN FLUFFY FRESH NEW CARPET'

It comes just days after Stacey and her three sons posed in her dressing room as she looked ahead to the birth of her baby girl.

Showing Zachary, Leighton and Rex touching her bump, she wrote: "Little Sister I don’t know much darling girl, but all I do know for sure little one is that your brothers will always be right behind you.

"I can not wait to watch you all grow together. I think about it every day. They love you so much already that they were more excited to feel you than the fluffy fresh new carpet and that’s saying something."

"We cannot wait to hold you little one. And your brothers are ready to big big brothers to a little sister," she concluded the emotional post.

Stacey could be giving birth any day now after she revealed her baby bump had "dropped" as they raced to finish renovations at Pickle Cottage.

Filming herself standing in her pyjamas and cradling her belly, the Loose Women panellist said she felt her baby girl had "really dropped down".

"In other news, I feel like she's really dropped down recently which is making me panic," Stacey began the video. "I don't want this to be over."
