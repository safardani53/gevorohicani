GARDEN lighting not only allows us to entertain and dine outdoors in the evenings, but it's also great for creating an ambience in any outdoor space.

Light up your garden, balcony, patio or porch with beautiful but affordable garden lanterns - we've rounded up 12 of the best available to buy now.

Garden lanterns are an easy way to light up your outdoors on the cheap, and there are many different types and styles available, with candlelight lanterns and solar lanterns among the most popular.

Solar lanterns automatically light up at night after their solar panels heat up from the sun during the day.

You'll find them in a variety of silhouettes to suit your space and aesthetics, like Moroccan-style filigree designs which cast beautiful patterns of light in the garden.

Rattan garden lanterns are another popular choice for a natural finish, while torch lanterns are easy to install in your plant beds.

When you need your garden lantern to do more than be a source of light, you'll also find garden lanterns that will help move buzzing insects away.

How to make a garden lantern

There are many ways to make your own garden lanterns.

One of the simplest ways is to make a design with holes in a tin can and put a tea light inside or you can put candles in wine bottles for easy garden lamps.

Empty jars of varying sizes can also create a warm, cosy feel grouped together with candles, bunched up fairy lights - or even glow sticks inside.
