THE History Channel's heart-stopping reality series Swamp People follows the day-to-day activities of alligator hunters.

The group of hunters lives in various swamp areas in south central Louisiana.

Who's in the cast of Swamp People?

Swamp People premiered on the History Channel in August 2010.

The hunters hunt American alligators for a living.

Since its start in 2010, the show's cast members are frequently changed. Check out the cast of hunters in season 14:

Who is new on Season 14 of Swamp People?

Swamp People often rotates through new cast members each season. This season, fans were introduced to Anna Ribbeck.

Anna Ribbeck is a social media strategist from Mandeville, Louisiana.

According to her LinkedIn, Ribbeck says she has "a passion for brand building and content creation. I have 6 years of social media expertise from the outdoor industry."

Ribbeck also enjoys archery, hunting, and jewelry-making in her downtime.

Her LinkedIn also points out that she is "an avid bowhunter and sponsored competitive archer whose mission is to increase archery participation by educating and promoting positive, ethical, and safe archery practices through my brand, AnnaTheArcher."

In 2018, Ribbeck appeared on the Wheel of Fortune.

Have any cast members been injured on the show?

During season four, Willie Edwards made a potentially fatal mistake while hunting gators.

If there's one thing alligator hunters should know is to be completely sure a gator is dead before sticking your hand in its mouth.

During the episode, Willie thought a gator was dead and got quite a painful shock when it bit down on his hand.

"He almost took my fingers off," Willie shouted after freeing his hand from the reptile's mouth.

After yanking his hand away - his middle, ring, and pinky fingers were bright red and spewing out blood.
