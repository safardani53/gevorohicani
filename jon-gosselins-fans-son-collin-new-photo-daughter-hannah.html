JON Gosselin's fans wondered where his troubled son Collin, 17, was as the reality star shared a new photo with daughter Hannah after their road trip to California.

Jon, 44, and Hannah, also 17, celebrated Thanksgiving together on Instagram.

In the snapshot, the father and daughter posed next to a staircase decorated with Christmas lights.

Hannah wore a long-sleeved white shirt with a California license on it. 

Jon covered up his head with a green cap and had on a plaid shirt. 

The two appeared to be in good spirits as they posed together for the family photo. 

The former TV star mentioned that the "holidays are a time for family and reflection."

Jon continued: "Be present and thankful today and every day."
