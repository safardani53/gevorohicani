KENDALL Jenner and her boyfriend, Devin Booker, were caught up in a fatal car accident, as the stars appeared devastated after witnessing the crash.

Kendall, 25, and basketball player Devin, 24, got out of their car in Biloxi, Mississippi last Saturday afternoon to see the cause of a traffic build-up.

In photos and video exclusively obtained by The Sun, Kendall had her hand over her mouth in shock over the scene when she got out of her black 4x4 to get a better look. 

The accident took place at 12:40pm when a man driving a truck pulling a tractor lost control of his vehicle and was thrown into the water of Biloxi Bay, according to police. 

Authorities added that when crossing the drawbridge grates, the vehicle and trailer began to spin out of control.

The driver side of the truck hit the west-side barrier of the bridge, ejecting the driver from the truck and over the barrier into the water.

Around 2:30pm, officials said they pulled the deceased man from the water.

He has still not been identified.

A passenger in the truck was injured and taken to a nearby hospital, where the person is in stable condition.

The southbound lanes of the freeway were closed while first responders searched for the man.

The model and NBA star were not involved in the accident, just caught up in the traffic close behind.

Kendall and Devin spent the morning before the accident opening up two renovated basketball courts in Moss Point Mississippi.

Devin was a basketball star at Moss Point High School before going pro and playing for the Phoenix Suns.
