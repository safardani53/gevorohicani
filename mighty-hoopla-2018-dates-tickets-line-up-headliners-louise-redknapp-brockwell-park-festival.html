When is Mighty Hoopla 2018? Dates, tickets, line-up and headliners with Louise Redknapp for the Broc

July 09, 2024
blog

MIGHTY Hoopla returns to London this summer and, with a new homes south of the river, it's set to be bigger and better than ever.

But who's in the line-up, and how do you get a ticket? Here are the deets...

Where can I get Mighty Hoopla tickets?

Tickets for the festival are available NOW from Ticketweb.

Act fast and you might still be able to nab an Early Bird ticket at the discounted price of £35.

Tickets will then increase to £40 and then £45 depending on when you book.

Alternatively, get your mates to join you and book six tickets for the price of five.

Note: no kids are allowed at Mighty Hoopla - it's for those aged 18 and over.

When and where is Mighty Hoopla?

This year Mighty Hoopla takes place on Sunday 3 June.

It's moved home from east London’s Victoria Park to Brockwell Park, near Brixton in south London.

Gates open at midday and the event finishes at 10.30pm.

More gig & festival ticket info

Who is performing at Mighty Hoopla 2018?

The line-up for Mighty Hoopla includes:

Louise

After touring the UK on a sold-out jaunt in January, Louise will hit the festival scene with a selection of her biggest hits.

Expect to hear Naked, In Walked Love and Arms Around The World.

After confirming she's been in the studio, fans will be hoping to hear some new music from Louise.

Lily Allen

The LDN singer will launch her pop comeback at Mighty Hoopla as she headlines the main stage.

Expect a set jam-packed with Lily's hits Smile, The Fear, Not Fair and Hard Out Here.

She's spent the past three years working on the follow up to 2014's Sheezus album.

Melanie C

While the world awaits a Spice Girls reunion tour, Melanie C fans can look forward to a solo set from the iconic girlband member.

With an illustrious solo career of her own, Melanie will be performing all of her hits including Never Be The Same Again, I Turn To You and Next Best Superstar.

Also on the bill are Belinda Carlisle, B*Witched, Louisa Johnson, MNEK and Vengaboys.

Plus, there's a "very special guest" still be be announced.
