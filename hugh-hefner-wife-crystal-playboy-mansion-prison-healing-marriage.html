HUGH Hefner’s wife Crystal called the Playboy Mansion “prison.”

The 35-year-old also said she’s still “healing” from her “contradicting” marriage.

Crystal opened up about her days as a Playboy bunny in a new Instagram post, which she shared on Thursday.

Alongside a snap of the model posing in front of the Playboy Mansion in 2016, she wrote: “I like this photo. It was toward the end when I started saying no to things that weren’t me.

“Hair got more natural, implants came out. No more fake tan or life.”

She continued: “I was still sick physically and mentally, but on the road to recovery.”

Crystal, who previously revealed that her breast implants "slowly poisoned” her in a 2016, added: “I’m always torn when I talk about the Playboy Mansion because in a lot of ways it was a sanctuary to me, but in other ways, it was my prison.”

She then reflected on her marriage to Hugh, writing: “He was good to me in many ways, but in other ways, he wasn’t.

Crystal wrote: "After I was diagnosed with Lyme disease and toxic mold, I posted about it on social media. I began to receive comments saying that my symptoms resembled 'Breast Implant Illness.'
