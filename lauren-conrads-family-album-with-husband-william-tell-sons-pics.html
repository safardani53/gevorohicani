Lauren Conrad’s tribe consists of her husband, William Tell,, and their two sons, Liam and Charlie — and she wouldn’t have it any other way.

The Laguna Beach alum wed the guitarist in September 2014 in California. “What a perfect way to start our lives together; surrounded by the people we love most,” the couple told Us Weekly in a statement at the time.

The pair welcomed their first son, Liam, in July 2017. His baby brother, Charlie Wolf, was born in October 2019.

After learning there were two other celebrity kids named Charlie Wolf — both Zooey Deschanel and Carly Waddell have sons with the same name — Conrad joked in November 2019, “It sounds like I’m a follower.”
