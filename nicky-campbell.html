Nicky Campbell Age, Biography

Nicky Campbell was born on April 10, 1961, in Edinburgh, Scotland. His nationality is Scottish, and he is of mixed ethnicity. He was adopted as a baby. His adoptive mother worked as a psychiatric social worker, and his adoptive father worked as a map printer. He went to the exclusive school Edinburgh Academy. Nicky revealed in July 2022 that he watched and endured sexual and brutal physical assault there, which had a “significant impact on [his] life.” He earned a 2:1 degree in history from the University of Aberdeen.

Nicky Campbell resolved to track down his Irish biological father in 2002. His birth mother was a Dublin Protestant, but his real father was a Northern Irish Catholic thirteen years his junior. He also learned that his grandpa was a member of the IRA from 1919 to 1921 and that his biological father was a member of the IRA in the 1950s and still had Irish Republican sentiments. Blue-Eyed Son [Story of an Adoption], his narrative of being adopted and tracing both his birth parents and extended family in Ireland, was published in 2004. Stella, his birth mother, died in 2007; Campbell spoke at her funeral in Dublin.

He announced on social media on December 12, 2019, that his mother, Sheila, had died at the age of 96. In 2007, she appeared on Who Do You Think You Are with her kid.
