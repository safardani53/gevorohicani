Size 24 and size 10 women try on same outfits from Boohoo's new spring range to see how they look on

April 11, 2024
blog

ON THE hunt for some new spring items to add to your wardrobe?

Well look no further as a group of four friends ranging from a size 10 to size 24 have tried on Boohoo's latest range to see what it really looks like on different body shapes.

Sharing the mega fashion haul on her YouTube channel, Debbie Crown and three of her friends started things off with a very colourful number.

Natasha, a size 24, and Debbie, Dee Dee and Deborah are all size 10 - they each tried on the abstract plisse cowl neck midi dress from Boohoo to start.

The colourful number looked stunning on all the girls however for the smaller girls the dress fitted a lot tighter compared to Natasha's.

Debbie said: "All of ours look tight on us, and yours looks so good, if you're plus size it's true to size."

For some of the women, the dress was so difficult to get into they revealed they wouldn't wear it again.

Next was a two toned knitted rib jumper and flared leggings set in green.

Debbi said it looked true to size but once the girls tried it on, not everyone was impressed by the two-piece set.

Debbie added: "It looks frumpy," while her friend's admitted it would look better if the top was cropped.

Natasha added that she would wear a white corset top with the trousers, which was a favourite with everyone.

They also tried an orange chiffon shirt with a twist detail in the centre - and it was a clear winner for all of the girls.

Deborah loved the fit of the shirt and Natasha liked the fact it covered her belly but had to size up to a 26 instead of her usual size 24.

The girls all grabbed some nude leggings as well, but Debbie pointed out straight away that the quality of the fabric felt bad.

"It's really thin so I feel like you'll be able to see whatever underwear your wearing through it, I like the design nevertheless," she said.

Debbie and Natasha found the leggings to be on the smaller size and recommended sizing up if you plan to buy it, but Deborah and Dee Dee both loved the leggings.

The girls also tried on an abstract pink copped shirt with a tie detail at the front.

The wrap-around top was a hit with all the women and Natasha added: It's a very mum fit, it's sexy but it's not too much."

The final item the girls got was a pink swimsuit with a belted waist and scooped back.

None of the girls were a fan of the swimsuit claiming it didn't look nice on their chest, and Natasha's didn't fit.

The video has already attracted over 8.000 views and people quickly took to the comments to share their thoughts.

One wrote: "The rainbow dress is so spring summer."

Another person commented: "That pink cropped shirt looked awesome on every one of you. It was so flattering and feminine!"

"The orange was my fave," penned a third.
