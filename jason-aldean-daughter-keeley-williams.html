JASON Aldean has found massive success as an American singer.

Now, fans want to know more about the country star's eldest child, Keeley Williams.

Who is Jason Aldean's daughter Keeley Williams?

Keeley Williams was born on February 14, 2003.

The 20-year-old is Jason Aldean's firstborn.

In May 2021, she graduated from Summit High School in Spring Hill, Tennessee, where she played volleyball.

Jason, 46, congratulated Keeley via a May 22, 2021 Instagram post.

Alongside a photo of his daughter, he wrote: "Hard to believe this kid graduated from high school tonight.

"Really excited to see what the next chapter in life holds for her. Your future is what u make it, so go get em Keeley. We love you!"

The former high school athlete is now a student at Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU).

On Valentine's Day 2021, Jason shared a birthday tribute for his firstborn on Instagram.

"I don’t think we realize how fast 18 years comes and goes. Keeley, I am so proud of you and the person you have grown into. Happy birthday kiddo. We love u so much! #valentinesbaby❤️."

How many kids does Jason Aldean have?

Jason has four kids, including three daughters and one son.

He shares daughters Keeley and Kendyl with his ex-wife, Jessica Ussery.

15-year-old Kendyl was born on August 20, 2007.

Jason shares his son Memphis and daughter Navy with his wife, 35-year-old Brittany Kerr Aldean, whom he wed in March 2015.

The pair welcomed Memphis, five, on December 2, 2017.

Navy, four, was born on February 4, 2019.

In January 2020, Jason spoke with Us Weekly about balancing his career and family life.

He said: "Now I’ve made it a point at this point of my career to scale back a little bit and still make sure that I’m out playing shows and doing what I need to do there, but spending a little more time at home too.

"I think it’s something that I’ve gotten a grip on a little, the older I’ve gotten and the longer I’ve been in the business. I think I’ve finally, after all these years, got it figured out now."

The star continued: "I still love what I do. I love playing shows and doing my thing, but in the early days of my career where I was playing 200 shows a year and was never at home, that kind of gets rough."

Who is Jason Aldean's ex-wife Jessica?

Jason was married to his high school sweetheart, Jessica, from 2001 to 2013.

Jessica was born on October 18, 1979, in Georgia.

The 43-year-old tied the knot with a man named Jake Marlin in 2015.

Jessica largely stays out of the public eye, keeping the details of her personal life private.

As Us Weekly reported, the former couple split in January 2013 after almost 12 years of marriage, with Jason filing for divorce in April that same year.

The country singer told Us Weekly at the time: "This is a really tough time for my entire family. Jessica and I have been together since we were teenagers. We’ve been through a lot of ups and downs over the years as we grew up together as a couple.

"She will always be important to me because she is the mother of my children, and I know that we will both always make our daughters our No. 1 priority."
