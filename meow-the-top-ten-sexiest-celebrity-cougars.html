THE Scottish Sun told this week how Rod Stewart’s son Sean is dating Real Housewives reality star Adrienne Maloof.

With a gap of 19 years between the pair, it looks like Sean has bagged himself a real-life cougar.

And he's not the only one.

We take a look at ten more famous older women who have snared younger beaus in a list of the top ten sizzling, sexiest celebrity cougars.

Carol Vorderman

The sultry Countdown and Loose Women presenter is one of the most recognised stars on British TV.

The 59-year-old is currently enjoying a relationship with Graham Duff, a stunt pilot for the Red Arrows.

Coming in at 14 years her junior, the age difference isn’t huge but it’s enough to make Vorders one of the hottest cougars on the list.


Melanie Sykes

49-year-old Melanie Sykes is definitely a yummy mummy, with two young kids Roman and Valentino to look after.

Now the TV presenter looks set to be a married cougar as she plans to wed her toyboy Jack Cockings who is fifteen years younger than her.

The two hooked up after exchanging flirty messages via Twitter in August of last year and are now preparing to tie the knot.


Demi Moore

Demi Moore is perhaps the best known celebrity cougar in the world.

The former Mrs Bruce Willis isn’t shy about her preference for younger men, especially after her marriage in 2005 to actor Ashton Kutcher.

The 57-year-old actress split from Kutcher, who was 15 years her junior, and has gone on to have another relationship with a much younger beau, namely Harry Morton.

Proving that age is just a number, Morton was younger than Kutcher, coming it at 31 making him 19 years younger than the sexy Striptease superstar.


Joan Collins

As an iconic diva of stage and screen, Joan Collins has never been without the attention of men, young and old.

Married five times in her 87 years, the Dynasty star got hitched most recently in 2001 to theatrical company manager Percy Gibson.

For age difference, Collins has a difference of 32 years between herself and Gibson proving that age is just a number when it comes to love.


Eva Longoria

Another serial cougar, Desperate Housewives star Eva Longoria has a long list of younger men who have seccumed to her scintillating charms.

The 45-year-old actress was married to NBA basketballer Tony Parker who was seven years younger than her, before they divorced in 2011.

However, Longoria didn’t wait around, instead sinking her claws into NFL quarterback Mark Sanchez, 12 years her junior.

The Hollywood bombshell has also been linked with Penelope Cruz’s younger brother Eduardo who is 11 years younger.


Madonna

Following her very public break up with husband Guy Ritchie, Madonna bounced back in true cougar style by bagging herself toyboy Jesus Luz.

There was a 19 year gap between the two, before they split up in 2010.

Not to be put off by the experience, the 61-year-old Queen of Pop went on to date dancer Brahim Zabat, 32, and the age gap between them doesn’t appear to be bothering either party in the slightest.


Kim Catrall

If there was ever an actress who got the line between her most famous part and reality mixed up, it’s Kim Catrall.

The Sex and the City actress was famous for playing man hungry Samantha in the hit TV show and it seems that the same ferocious appetite for younger men spilled over into her personal life.

Married three times, 63-year-old Catrall has also dated actor Alexander Siddig, nine years her junior, along with rumoured relationships with basketball stars Steve Francis, 43, and Cuttino Mobley, 44.


Kelly Brook

While she may be one of the junior members of this list, Kelly Brook has still enjoyed a number of high profile relationships with men much younger than her.

The 40-year-old model and Celebrity Juice star once had a relationship with Danny Cipriani who was eight years younger than her.

When that relationship broke down, Brook moved onto Scotland rugby ace Thom Evans, 28.

Although the gap has been reduced to five years, Brook looks set to follow in her fellow cougar footsteps.


Jennifer Lopez

Jennifer Lopez is still one of the biggest music and acting talents in the world.

When she split from husband Marc Anthony in 2011, J-Lo went straight for her backing dancer Casper Smart who is 18 years younger than her.

The Latino singer, who is already a yummy mummy of twins, is still going strong with her young man as the two look to be starting a family of their own.

Kylie Minogue

Pint sized Australian pop princess Kylie Minogue has been dating her toyboy for almost five years.

The 51-year-old Ozzie and supermodel partner Andres Velencoso Segura don’t seem to mind the 10 year age gap between them and have been going strong since 2008.

Latest rumours have suggested that the couple might be tying the knot, proving that young men and cougar relationships can work out.
