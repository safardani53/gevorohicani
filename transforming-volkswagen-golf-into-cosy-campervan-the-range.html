A DRIVER has revealed how to transform your vehicle into a cosy campervan using items from The Range.

Ave Marin, an experienced car camper, shocked vehicle owners with the transformation of her Volkswagen Golf into a snug retreat.

In a video uploaded onto TikTok, she shows just how to completely change your car into the perfect place to stay overnight.

By emptying the boot of her car and lowering the seats, Ave was able to make plenty of room for two people to spend the night in.

However, what really made it sleepable and cosy was the use of blankets, pillows and a memory foam topper to build a makeshift bed.

Attached to the 24 second video on her TikTok account (@greenybeany420) was a caption that read: "She's cozy and we love her."

Some added text in the video also said: "POV: You have a VW Golf but still love to car camp."

The video became very popular with fellow car owners and camping lovers, with many of them shocked at how well Ave managed to transform the back of her car.

One person said: "This legit looks SO COZY."

However, Ave also provided some useful insight to those with burning questions.

In response to one person who asked how they manage with airflow, the TikToker replied: "We crack the windows and have a little fan for fresh air!"

Interestingly, Ave did reveal that the back of the car isn't as flat as people think.

After one viewer said their back seats don't fold all the way down, Ave said that neither do hers and that it remains a little slanted.

In order to overcome this issue, though, she revealed that she uses a full size 4” memory foam mattress topper.

Along with the blankets and pillows, similar products can be purchased from The Range.
