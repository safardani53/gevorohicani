Melanie Hamrick Sunbathes By The Pool In A Black Bikini Hollywood Life

June 18, 2024

Melanie Hamrick, who became the mother of Mick Jagger‘s eighth and youngest child in 2016, took a break from mommy duty to soak up plenty of sunshine in her July 31 Instagram post. The 34-year-old choreographer has been dating the Rolling Stones rocker, 79, since 2014, so she has become accustomed to his busy lifestyle, but this week, she took some time to slip into a bikini and lounge by a pool.

Melanie and Mick started dating each other in 2014, shortly after the Rolling Stones rocker’s girlfriend of 13 years, L’Wren Scott, died. In 2016, Melanie became the mother of Mick’s eighth child, 5-year-old son Deveraux, who — even with blonde curls — looks like the spitting image of his father. Melanie put her dance career on hold while carrying Mick’s baby and officially retired in 2019, so she’s now focused on being a full-time mom. But as we said, she took a break from mommy duty to enjoy pool time this week.

In the first photo seen above, Melanie faced towards the pool and away from whoever was taking the gorgeous photos of her. Then, she laid back and grabbed ahold of a glass of wine in the second photo. The third and final image showed Melanie looking directly at the camera while she stretched out her body sitting on the chair.

The gorgeous brunette captioned her post, “Summer days ☀️.” While Mick didn’t comment on his girlfriend’s post, his 23-year-old son Lucas Jagger did have something to say. Lucas, who Mick shares with his ex-girlfriend Luciana Gimenez, commented, “lifestyle,” on Melanie’s post, seemingly complimenting her chill day in the sun.

In May 2021, Melanie brought her baby boy to a Peter Rabbit 2 screening in London with Mick’s Rolling Stones bandmate Ronnie Wood‘s wife Sally Wood and their twin daughters Gracie Jane and Alice Rose. Little Deveraux looked a lot like his dad Mick at the event as he donned a cute outfit that included a light blue button-down patterned top and gray pants.

All together, Mick has eight kids with five different women, and he makes sure he spends quality time with all of them pretty regularly. Along with Deveraux and Lucas, Mick is the father to Karis Jagger, his daughter with Marsha Hunt, and Jade Jagger, his daughter with ex-wife Bianca. Mick and his ex-girlfriend Jerry Hall also have four children together: Lizzie, James, Georgia May and Gabriel.
