Take a trip down memory lane with photos throughout Kelly’s entire incredible career!

Kelly Clarkson was just 20 years old when she was crowned the winner of American Idol in 2002, and her career has taken off in massive ways ever since. The talented singer has now been in the industry for 20 years, and things have changed quite a bit for her in the years since the show! Not only has Kelly grown up in front of our eyes, but she started a family, and began ventures like her own talk show and working as a coach on The Voice. While Kelly is still the same lovable person who viewers fell in love with on ‘American Idol,’ she’s definitely transformed in so many ways during her years in the spotlight.
