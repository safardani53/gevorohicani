The best high street wedding dresses for every budget, from 100 to 600

April 09, 2024
blog

IT'S one of the biggest days of your life and you want your outfit to be perfect.

But perfection doesn’t always have to cost a fortune

Many high street retailers are now selling their own bridal ranges for those who don’t quite have the cash for couture.

Whether you’re a traditional or alternative bride, you’re sure to find something to suit you.

We've scoured the shops to find the stand out stylse available now - and prices start for as little as £100.

From full length dresses to classy jumpsuits, check out our favourites from the high street.

The Bardot Dress

From Dorothy Perkins’ Showcase range comes this stunning ivory dress, which cost £110.

Perfect for a summer wedding, the off-the-shoulder design will keep you cool on a hot day.

The gorgeous lace bodice and satin waistband adds a touch of elegance.

Pair with an up-do to sweep hair away from your face and show off that smile.

The Vintage Dress

  • Ivory 'Bella' Bardot Wedding Dress, £150, Dorothy Perkins - buy here

If maxi dresses aren’t your thing, this 1950’s gown from Dorothy Perkins will be right up your street.

It usually cost £150 but is currently available for just £75 - a 50 per cent discount.

Falling just below the knee, this all over lace number is ideal for the vintage-loving bride.

A scalloped hem and neckline add beautiful detail to the dress and skirt underlayers add volume for a classic silhouette.

Complete the look with statement heels and drop earrings.

The Embellished Dress

  • Phase Eight - Ivory hope wedding dress, £595, Debenhams - buy here

Add a little sparkle to your day with this hand-embroidered Phase Eight wedding dress.

This full-length gown is covered in shimmering beads and sequins so you can make a dazzling entrance.

A figure skimming fit can suit a more relaxed wedding while also flattering the bride.

With a sweetheart neckline and short train, this is the ultimate romantic wedding dress.

The Silver Dress

Who said wedding dresses have to be white?

If you’re less of a traditional bride, opt for this silver maxi dress from Debut.

With beautiful crystal embellishments and open back detailing, you can truly stand out on your big day.

And at just £96 down from £120, it’s an absolute steal.

The On Trend Dress

  • Meara Lace Sleeve High Low Hem Dress White - £68, Very - buy here

Make a statement with this dramatic dip hem dress from Chi Chi London.

This bang on trend beauty is a gorgeous alternative to the traditional full-length dress.

Featuring a button-up back, sheer lace inserts and a sweetheart neckline, this £68 winner is sure to wow the one you love.

The Jumpsuit

If dresses aren’t your thing, try this elegant jumpsuit from Ted Baker.

Go bold on your wedding day and stay comfortable with a casual fit that will help you strut your stuff on the dancefloor.

The one shoulder frill and embellished belt creates a sophisticated and feminine feel.

And if you can’t wait to try this on, you can pick it up for free with Click and Collect within two days of ordering.
