Jennifer Lopez Just Wore Free People Parachute Pants

August 04, 2024
blog

Don’t be fooled by the rocks that she’s got — Jennifer Lopez’s laid-back look reminds us that she’s still Jenny from the block.

The singer and actress, 54, stepped out in Malibu this weekend draped in a nearly head-to-toe Free People ‘fit, elevated with a pair of see-through heels, a beige clutch, gold sunglasses, and layered necklaces. Though her look was a master class in comfy-casual style, the main attraction was her baggy parachute pants. 

The best part? These Free People pants were one of the most affordable aspects of her transitional fall outfit. You can even nab her off-the-shoulder sweater from Free People, too, though her exact shade is been sold out. (Luckily, there are ten others to choose from.) 

Free People To The Sky Parachute Pants, $78

The parachute pants Lopez opted for were made from a baggy cotton blend in a muted green hue, though there are eight other shades available if olive isn’t your thing. Drawstring and closure-free, the billowy pants are just as comfortable as they are stylish. They even come with pockets, which gives this pair extra brownie points in our book. You can style them with an off-the-shoulder top like Lopez, or grab a cable-knit sweater for a fall look that screams Gilmore Girls.

In the name of bargain buying, we also found more budget-friendly options of the J.Lo-approved pants, which just may become your new replacement for jeans.

Parachute Pants Inspired by Jennifer Lopez

Onirike Parachute Pants, $25 (Save 24%)

If J.Lo’s pair feels a bit too Malibu for your liking, then we think these Y2K-inspired cargo pants are a great alternative. They have an oversized design that is cinched by drawstrings at the waistline and hems, accentuating its parachute style while still giving the silhouette some shape. 

One five-star reviewer who called them the “best pants ever” commended the design. “The drawstrings at the waist and ankles make the fit customizable for any look,” they wrote. Available in 20 shades, you’re bound to find a hue that complements your wardrobe. 

Anrabess Wide-Leg Palazzo Pants, $22 with Prime

But if you prefer the laid-back California look that Lopez was draped in, then consider channeling her style with these billowy linen palazzo pants. The dark green pants have the same wide-leg silhouette as her Free People pair, and they come with deep side pockets and a drawstring waist to help you achieve that slouchy look. A shopper raved about how comfortable the pants are, calling them “pants you can live in.”

However, some left reviews saying that this pair runs a little large, so we’d suggest sizing down for a tighter fit — but the drawstring will help if there’s too much gapping at your waist. Whether you like to wear your parachute pants oversized or more snug, this trendy style (and one of the 13 color options) will fit your fancy. 

Artfree Cargo Parachute Pants, $26 (Save 22%)

Leaning back into the nostalgic style of the early aughts is this pair of cargo parachute pants from Amazon. The pants are machine washable and not quite stretchy, but are definitely comfortable due to their loose fit. One shopper loved how easy it was to pair them with “slides, sneakers, or heels.” 

Tba Lantern Tapered Pants, $16 (Save 18%)

Channel a ’70s vibe in these capri pants, which have a loose-fitting silhouette, tortoise buttons for extra pizzazz, and deep front pockets for your keys and phone. And did we mention that this pair is super budget-friendly, too? They usually cost $19, but right now, you can snag them for just $16.
