A FOODIE has shared a mouth-watering salt and pepper chips recipe using an air fryer.

Stephanie, better known as Not So Farmers Wife on TikTok, shared the recipe online - and it's so simple, it's bound to become a favourite in your household.

For this, the mum demonstrated in the video, you will need chips, 1/2 red bell pepper 1/2 green bell pepper, onion and a mixture of spices.

To give the chips a bit of a kick, the foodie went for 1/2 tsp of garlic powder, 1 1/2 tsp of Chinese five spice, as well as 1/3 tsp of chilli flakes.

Once ready with the ingredients, simply chuck them inside your air fryer for 20 minutes at 200C.

Stephanie, who has close to 27k fans on the social media giant, also advised to give the chips a stir halfway through.
