Migos' Takeoff seen in haunting last picture just minutes before rapper was shot dead in Houston bow

September 17, 2024
blog

RAPPER Takeoff shared a haunting last post before he was shot dead at a private party in Houston.

The Migos star, 28, shared a final picture to his Instagram account on Tuesday evening with the lyrics to the song Stop Breathing.

Shortly after, he was gunned down at a bowling alley in the Texas city.

Takeoff's bandmate and uncle Quavo reportedly rushed to help him after TMZ reports the star was shot in either the head or the neck.

Cops later confirmed both stars were at the party. Officers were first called around 2:30am in the early hours of Wednesday.

Quavo, who wasn't injured in the shooting, can be heard screaming for help, according to footage seen TMZ.

It's believed the pair were playing dice when the shooting happened.

Two others have been taken to hospital and around 40-50 people were at the private party.

It's not known what sparked the shooting.

Quavo was seen in a car with Jas Prince - the CEO of the Young Empire Music Group, shortly before the fatal shooting.

Takeoff, full name Kirshnik Khari Ball, was part of the hip-hop trio Migos alongside his uncle Quavo and cousin Offset.

The music star, from Georgia, started rapping in 2008 and Migos released their first mixtape in August 2011.

The group was known for their track Versace and achieved their first number-one single Bad and Boujee in 2016.

Takeoff recently shared a music video of his and Quavo's new track Messy after Offset left the group.

He also starred at Lil Weezyana Fest which was held in New Orleans on October 29.

The lineup included Lil Wayne and Drake made a surprise appearance.

Following his death, artists have flooded Takeoff's Instagram page with worried comments as they prayed the "news wasn't true".

Lil Pump said: "God plz tell me this ain't true."
