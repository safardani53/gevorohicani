Tito Ortiz is a renowned American mixed martial artist who was born on January 23, 1975. Recently, he signed a contract with Combate Americas promotion and is mostly known for his stints with the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He had received a Light Heavyweight Champion from 2000 to 2003.

Moreover, he is also recognized as the CEO of Punishment Athletes MMA equipment and clothing line. He also has an MMA training gym named Punishment Training Center in his hometown, Huntington Beach, California. It was November 5, 2020, when he was announced as one of the winners of the Huntington Beach City Council election.
