REAL Housewives of Beverly Hills alum Lisa Rinna has gone over to the dark side in her latest experimentation with fashion.

The ex-Bravo personality appeared at the 2023 Paris Fashion Week swallowed in ruffles, and her large lips heavily glossed.

The unconventional jet-black trench coat that Lisa, 59, sported at the esteemed event had deep-pleated ruffles along the sleeves, neckline, and coming down the outwear's pocket.

Lisa wore skintight black leggings with the coat - the jacket was used in lieu of an actual top.

She paired the outfit with chic shiny black leather boots, topping the entire ensemble off with black alien sunglasses that covered the majority of her slim face.

With her hair pulled back into an updo and her shades filling up half her face, Lisa's lips were the standout feature.

INJECTED

Permanent lip fillers are to blame for Lisa's lips.

In 2020, Rinna released her own lip line called Rinna Beauty and revealed if she's had plastic surgery or not.

During an interview with DailyMail.com, she explained that she got permanent silicone filler lip injections when she was 24 years old in order to help her brand.

"I always say, jokingly, that my lips have a career of their own," she said at the time.

"The feedback on them was not always positive. There was a time when people were negative. But I think they became accepted when I said, 'These are my lips! This is the way they are.'"

Rinna went on to then explain that after 10 years, the silicone started to seep through her lips, causing scar tissue.

As a result, she went back under the knife with Beverly Hills plastic surgeon Garth Fisher, who reconstructed her lip by removing damaged tissue, and she has reportedly been happy ever since.

BYE-BYE TO HOUSEWIVES

Now that Lisa is no longer on reality television, she'll be able to dedicate more of her time to her budding business.

She officially announced her exit from Bravo's RHOBH in January.

Lisa shared a statement with PEOPLE about leaving RHOBH, saying: "This is the longest job I have held in my 35-year career, and I am grateful to everyone at Bravo and all those involved in the series.

"It has been a fun eight-year run, and I am excited for what is to come!"

Lisa's decision to leave the show came after her contract with the network expired at the end of last season.

According to PEOPLE, the soap opera actress and business mogul weighed her options against her business obligations, and she and Bravo mutually agreed it would be best not to return.

She joined the franchise in 2014 during the fifth season of the series.

Lisa found herself at the center of drama in her Real Housewives debut, getting involved in a heated argument with then-cast member Kim Richards.
