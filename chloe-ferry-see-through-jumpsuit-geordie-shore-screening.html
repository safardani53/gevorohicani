CHLOE Ferry has left little to the imagination after turning up to a Geordie Shore screening in a daring see-through jumpsuit.

Despite the plunging temperatures and snow flurries, the 28-year-old reality star made sure all eyes were on her thanks to the risqué outfit.

Chloe was snapped arriving at the event at London's Soho Hotel and didn't seem fazed by the freezing weather outside.

Instead, the brunette star slipped into a black pair of knickers with and bra before donning the lace jumpsuit.

She also walked the red carpet in a pair of see-through sandals and with her long dark hair flowing around her shoulders.

At one point, Chloe turned away from the cameras to give them a flash of her bottom, which was showing straight through the lace.

The event was held by MTV to launch the 24th series of Geordie Shore, which has been labelled the most explosive yet thanks to a series of fiery on-screen rows.

It was hosted by Kiss FM star Tyler West and featured most of the cast, including Chloe's girl pals Marnie Simpson, Abbie Holborn, Chantelle Connelly and Sophie Kasaei.

The lads were there too, with Nathan Henry standing out in a lime green suit jacket.

His co-stars James Tindale, Ricci Guarnaccio, Jay Gardner and Kyle Christie were also pictured on the red carpet at the five-star hoteL.

There was a certain famous face among the attendees too, with Katie Price attempting to steal the show in a cobalt blue catsuit.

The former glamour model had arrived earlier in the evening to get ready with Chloe before posing up a storm for the waiting photographers once there.

Katie, 45, then sidled up to the rest of the Geordie Shore girls for a snap.

Meanwhile, Chloe had travelled down to London from Newcastle on the train so she could attend the screening.

And despite managing to shrug off the cold weather for the launch, she struggled in the snow flurries as she walked to her hotel.

Wearing a coat, jumper and giant hoop earrings, she said to her followers on Instagram: "We've just arrived in London and it is starting to snow. Can you believe it?

"I'm absolutely freezing. Oh my God, it's so cold. It's physically snowing in London!"

Notably absent from the Geordie Shore screening was Charlotte Crosby.

Charlotte had joined the series with her fiancé Jake Ankers and their one-year-old daughter Alba, but she quit early into filming after a series of rows.
